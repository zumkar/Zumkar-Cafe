About us

Nestled in the heart of Gelephu Rabdaling, Zumkar Café is a cozy retreat for coffee lovers, food enthusiasts, and anyone looking for a warm, welcoming atmosphere. Founded with a passion for quality and community, we offer a curated menu of freshly brewed coffee, handcrafted beverages, and a variety of delicious snacks and meals made from locally sourced ingredients. Whether you’re dropping by for a quick bite or settling in for a relaxed gathering, Zumkar Café is your go-to destination for comfort, flavor, and friendly service. We take pride in being a part of the Gelephu community and strive to create a space where everyone feels at home.
